Canadian Fuel efficiency

Completed

What is this project about?

In this project, me and my teammate used R programming to analyze data
from the released information about fuel efficiency of various vehicles that
were released for 2025 models. We used city fuel efficiency to predict highway
fuel efficiency.

The data set was retrieved from: 2025 Fuel Consumption Ratings

What is my role in this project?

My role in this project was significant as I coded all the data into RSTUDIO,
analyzed the given data, and drew conclusions from the analyzed data.
I was also responsible for all the transformations, drawing the final conclusion.
Unfortunatley my partner contributed very minimally to this project as they
were responsible for writing up parts d, e, and f in the document.

Start: January 2025
Finish: WIP (Expected finish date: May 2025)

What is Twisted desires?

Twisted Desires is a eerie 2-D pixel game set in a highschool.
The player is a highschool boy who is being chased by his crazy
obssessive stalker. The player must use their wits and skills
to survive and escape the stalker throughout the school as they finds
keys and clues to unlock all the rooms in order to
find the master key which unlocks the exit.

   

What is my role in this project?

Since I work with 3 other people in this project, the roles and
responsibilities are dividing equally amongst myself and my 3 teammates, so far I have been
responsible for creating the classrooms, connecting the scenes, making and connecting the music to
the scenes and some other roles that have not been decided yet.
We are using the Unity to make this game.
