What is Twisted desires?

Twisted Desires is a eerie 2-D pixel game set in a highschool.
The player is a highschool boy who is being chased by his crazy
obssessive stalker. The player must use their wits and skills
to survive and escape the stalker throughout the school as they finds
keys and clues to unlock all the rooms in order to
find the master key which unlocks the exit.
